Community Service for Statistical Analysis

We provide statistical services for a non-profit organization located in Calgary. The workflow of this service as below:

  1. Please contact Chel via <imstatsbee@gmail.com> Filling out the intake form and email the form to one of organizers (we are currently preparing the intake form)

  2. You are going to be invited to give a background information about the problem of interest for 15 minutes.

  3. A special task team is made up of a small group of volunteers.

  4. An analysis is carried out for three months (by default) up to six months.

  5. The project is listed under the ongoing projects section.

  6. You are presenting the outcome.

Note that a service time cannot be asked to be shorten and a quality of service cannot be guaranteed since the only available resources are personal time and efforts from volunteers.

CalgaryR

CalgaryR is a community of data enthusiasts and endeavors working with R. We are pursuing a continuing education and creating a self-development opportunity from experiences shared by our friends. Chel Hee Lee, Cliff Sobchuk, Jonathan Lin, Dylan Loader, Adames Pablo, Jennifer Lee, John Brosz, and Byron Chu are volunteered to organize events. If you are interested in sponsoring our community, hosting a meeting, or making sugggestions, please feel free to contact one of the organizers. Feedback or question via email to Chel is of course most appreciated. Please join our Meetup and public workspace on Slack.

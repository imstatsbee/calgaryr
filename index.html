CalgaryR


Information on this site is frequently changed without any notification.

CalgaryR is a community of R users living in Calgary. This community aims to get people together from industry and academia in order to share experience for various statistical and analytical tasks using R. Chel Hee Lee, Marc Boulet, Cliff Sobchuk, Sonbol Yazdanbakhsh, Laura Keating and Nima Safaian are volunteered to organize events. If you are interested in sponsoring our community or hosting a meeting, please contact one of organizers. Feedback or question via email to Chel is of course most appreciated. Please join our Meetup and public workspace on Slack.
